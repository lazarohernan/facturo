Privacy Policy - Facturo

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

At Facturo, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name and email: To create and manage your account
  • Billing information: Customer data, products, services
  • Payment information: Processed through App Store (we don't store cards)
  • Business information: Company name, address, tax information

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device data: Device type, operating system
  • Usage data: How you use the app, features used
  • Location data: Only if you explicitly enable it
  • Identifiers: User ID, authentication tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide the Service

  • Create and manage invoices
  • Store customer information
  • Process payments through App Store
  • Send service notifications

2.2 To Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Identify technical issues
  • Develop new features
  • Optimize performance

3. Sharing Information

3.1 We Don't Sell Your Information

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information only with:

  • Apple: For payment processing and authentication
  • Supabase: For secure data storage
  • Email providers: For service notifications

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access control: Authorized personnel only
  • Regular audits: Security verifications
  • Standard compliance: Following best practices

4.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you request deletion
  • Billing data: Per legal requirements (5-10 years)
  • Technical data: 24 months for analysis
  • Deleted data: Permanent deletion within 30 days

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Basic Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data we have
  • Correct: Update incorrect information
  • Delete: Delete your account and data
  • Port: Export your data
  • Object: Limit processing

5.2 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights:

  1. Log in to the app
  2. Go to Profile > Account > Delete Account
  3. Follow the instructions
  4. Or contact us: facturohn@gmail.com

5.3 Account Deletion

  • Process: Profile > Account > Delete Account
  • Deleted data: All your personal information
  • Timeline: Permanent deletion within 30 days
  • Exceptions: Legally required data
